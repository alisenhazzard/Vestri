About

At a Higher Level is a podcast dedicated to all things related to video games and data viz, including books, interviews, and other awesome resources.

Check out the show notes for each episode for links to other great content, and to find out more about the topics from the episode.

Erik Hazzard is a video game designer, full-stack web developer, co-founder of Five.com, data-viz enthusiast, bearded he-man, and awesome guy who loves to talk about and share his interests with others.
